About PostGuard

With the PostGuard add-on, you can easily send and receive secure emails within your own email application. With PostGuard, your emails and files are end-to-end encrypted. This means that only intended recipients (and the sender) can decrypt and read the emails.
You can send PostGuarded emails to everybody you wish. This includes people that do not (yet) have the PostGuard add-on. They can either install the add-on or use the website postguard.eu/decrypt to access protected emails.
When you receive a PostGuarded email, you need to use the IRMA app to prove that you really (truly) are the intended recipient of the email. IRMA is a separate privacy-friendly authentication app (which is used also for other authentication purposes).

About us

PostGuard is being developed by a multidisciplinary team from iHub at Radboud University, in the Netherlands, funded by NWO. The aim is to foster secure communication by offering free email encryption based on advanced (identity-based) encryption techniques. The team focuses on usability and accessibility of encryption technology by combing expertise in computing science, security, and user experience design. This project partners with several public and private organisations in the Netherlands, including Nedap Healthcare, VGZ, SURFnet, the Municipality of Nijmegen and Compumatica.
